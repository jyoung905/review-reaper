# Review Reaper Business Plan

## One-line business
Review Reaper helps local service businesses turn bad public reviews into professional, owner-approved response drafts and recovery actions.

## Core problem
Local businesses get damaging 1–3 star reviews and often either:
- ignore them,
- reply defensively,
- reply with generic boilerplate,
- or do not know what pattern the bad reviews are revealing.

That hurts conversion because prospects read bad reviews before booking/calling.

## Target customer
Best first niches:
1. Dental clinics
2. Med spas / cosmetic clinics
3. Auto repair shops
4. Chiropractors / physio clinics
5. Restaurants with reputation-sensitive booking flow

Best first buyer:
- Owner/operator
- Office manager
- Clinic manager
- Reputation/marketing manager for multi-location businesses

Avoid for MVP:
- Huge chains with procurement cycles
- Businesses with no visible contact route
- Businesses with only one-off complaints and no pattern

## Offer
### MVP offer: Done-for-you review response desk
For **$97/month**, Review Reaper monitors public reviews and provides response drafts for negative reviews.

Customer gets:
- Monthly review scan
- Drafted responses for each new negative review
- Complaint theme summary
- Suggested recovery actions
- Optional edited tone after customer feedback
- Human approval before anything is posted

Important: Review Reaper does **not** automatically post to Google. It drafts; customer approves/copies/posts.

## First-sale positioning
The cold email should not ask them to immediately buy a subscription.

Better CTA:
> I can send a free mini-audit with the negative reviews, the complaint themes, and 3 response drafts. Want me to send it over?

Then sales flow:
1. Prospect replies yes.
2. We send mini-audit.
3. Offer: “I can monitor this monthly and send response drafts for $97/mo.”
4. If they say yes, send Stripe link.
5. After payment, collect onboarding details and start service.

## Why this is better
The current email-to-Stripe jump is too cold. They have not seen enough value yet.
A free mini-audit creates trust and gives them something useful before asking for money.

## Fulfillment model
### Manual MVP
At first, this can be mostly manual behind the scenes:
1. Find public review profile.
2. Capture negative review snippets.
3. Generate response drafts.
4. Review/edit for tone and risk.
5. Send audit/report by email.
6. If paid, repeat monthly or weekly depending plan.

### Productized later
Build customer portal only after validating demand.
Early customers can be managed through email + internal dashboard.

## Pricing
Initial price: **$97/month**

Includes:
- 1 business location
- Negative review monitoring
- Up to 10 response drafts/month
- Monthly complaint summary

Possible future tiers:
- $97/mo — single location, monthly monitoring
- $197/mo — weekly monitoring + more drafts
- $497/mo — multi-location / agency-style reputation desk

## Success metric
Early traction is not “subscribers from cold traffic.”
Better early metrics:
- Replies to cold email
- Prospects who request mini-audit
- Prospects who book/ask questions after mini-audit
- First paid monthly customer

## Current gap
The business should not scale outbound until we have:
- mini-audit template
- reply handling SOP
- onboarding form/page
- fulfillment checklist
- sample report
- clear customer success email sequence
